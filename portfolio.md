# Blurst.com (2007-2009)
Work at indie video game developer Flashbang Studios. In two years with a core team of six, we produced seven arcade-style score-attack games and three iPhone games, primarily in Unity3D.

Physics meets Puzzle Bobble, with a built-in level editor.
